The Journey of
Sa-Young

From the vibrant rhythms of Nigeria to the creative heart of Victoria, BC.

The Fusion of Afrobeat and Alté

Sa-Young is a visionary artist and producer who has spent years perfecting a sound that bridges continents. Born and raised in the rich musical landscape of West Africa, he brought those deep-rooted Afrobeat traditions to Canada, where they met the experimental, genre-bending influences of the Alté scene.

Victoria's Own Afro-Twist

Now based in Victoria, BC, Sa-Young is a prominent figure in the West Coast's emerging fusion scene. His performances are more than just concerts; they are immersive cultural experiences that combine live instrumentation, digital production, and a raw, soulful vocal delivery that he calls "Afro-Twist."

Whether performing at local festivals like Blastfest or concluding his Canada-wide tours, Sa-Young remains dedicated to his tribe—the listeners who crave authenticity, groove, and a sound that refuses to be categorized.

"Music is the thread that keeps us connected to where we came from, even as we explore where we're going."

— Sa-Young
