About Artist

Who is Sa-Young?

Sa-Young is an Afro-fusion artist crafting music where rhythm meets reflection, blending Afrobeats, Alté, and global influences. His sound, which he calls Afro-Twist, is built on percussive grooves, melodic storytelling, and a deep connection to culture and identity.

His music moves between two worlds: songs that make you dance, and songs that make you think.

After nearly a decade away from the spotlight, Sa-Young returned with renewed clarity and purpose, releasing two EPs in two years and re-establishing his voice through vibrant, intentional music.

He has shared stages with Afrobeats icons Kizz Daniel, Chike, and Buju, and performed at Blastfest Seattle alongside Adekunle Gold, Wande Coal, and Odumodublvck.

He also performs with The Tribe Band, delivering live experiences that blend the energy of Afrobeats with the richness of live instrumentation.
